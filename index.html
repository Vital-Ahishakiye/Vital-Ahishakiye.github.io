Understanding Unemployment in Rwanda

Public Sentiment Analysis of Rwanda's Distance-Based Fare Pricing Model

Analysis shows that ~50% of public comments were neutral or confused, indicating limited understanding of the new fare model. We recommend that future policy rollouts be supported by clear, accessible public education to build trust and ensure smoother adoption.
